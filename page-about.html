    About our web design & development services

    Do you include SEO with web design?

    Yes — every build ships with technical SEO best practices: semantic HTML, schema markup, XML sitemaps, clean URLs, and Core Web Vitals optimization. Content SEO and link-building are add-ons.

    Can you migrate my existing site without losing rankings?

    We handle audits, 301 redirects, canonicalization, and structured data to preserve (and often improve) your organic visibility during redesigns or platform moves.

    What stacks do you work with?

    Primarily PHP/MySQL with modern front-end tooling; we integrate APIs, dashboards, and CMSes as needed.

    Do you offer ongoing support?

    Yes — care plans include content updates, uptime monitoring, analytics reporting, and performance/SEO improvements.
