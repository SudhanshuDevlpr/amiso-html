      FAQs

      Common questions about our services

      How long does a typical website project take?

      Most small to mid-size websites take 3–6 weeks depending on scope, feedback cycles, and content readiness. Complex web apps can take 8–16 weeks.

      Do you provide SEO as part of web development?

      Yes. Every build includes SEO fundamentals—semantic markup, meta tags, XML sitemaps, schema markup, and performance optimizations that help with Core Web Vitals.

      Can you work with our existing CMS or tech stack?

      We frequently extend and optimize existing PHP/MySQL stacks and integrate REST/GraphQL APIs. We can also migrate legacy sites to modern architectures.

      What industries do you serve?

      We work with startups and SMEs across D2C, B2B services, logistics, education, and professional services throughout India.
